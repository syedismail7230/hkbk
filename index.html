Welcome to CMICIIE

CM Ibrahim Center for Innovation Incubation and Entrepreneurship

Get Started

HKBK Technology business incubator to nurture NEXTGEN entrepreneurs.

CMICIIE aims to produce 21st century entrepreneurs by instilling entrepreneurial spirit of India on the philosophy of churning global entrepreneurs locally. The center focuses on spearheading Innovation, Incubation and entrepreneurship contributing to government initiatives like Start-up India, Make in India and Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav

  • Startup ecosystem.
  • Developed and scalable business model.
  • Financial initiatives.

21st century, specifically in India, with its younger population, skilled and aspirational requires creating opportunities to participate optimally in the market. The market, which is likely to ride on financialization, internet, embedded technologies, agribusiness, etc., will bring about fundamental transformations in the way a business is set up and run. India is a slow starter in setting up facilities such as Incubators to promote a culture of entrepreneurship, incubate startups and actively contribute to product, service, and online businesses.

FAQ

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • WHO IS ELIGIBLE TO BECOME AN INCUBATOR CLIENT?

    A team, with at least two persons with full time commitment as founders, preferably with complementary skills, are eligible to apply. Founders with less than 18 years will be considered with stated consent of parents. Founding team with the following attributes or team composition will be encouraged.

  • HOW ARE INCUBATOR CLIENTS SELECTED?

    Refer the selection criteria in policy.

  • HOW LONG CAN MY COMPANY REMAIN IN THE INCUBATOR?

    The goal of CMICIIE Incubator Network is to support companies in a way that will enable the business to graduate from the incubator in 12 to 14 months from date of Incubation.

  • WHAT KINDS OF MILESTONES AM I REQUIRED TO MEET?

    The CMICIIE Entrepreneurial services team will review the progress toward these milestones with the business throughout the year.

  • WHAT HAPPENS IF MY COMPANY OUTGROWS THE INCUBATOR OR IS READY TO GRADUATE?

    If the business outgrows or graduates from the incubator, the CMICIIE team will assist the company in locating a new space, either within the CMICIIE Regional Incubator Network or at another appropriate site. If the business meets the graduation criteria ahead of schedule, it may be able to terminate the current aggrement, at the discretion of CMICIIE management.
